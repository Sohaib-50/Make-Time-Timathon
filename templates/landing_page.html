Does your life feel boring? When you think of the past few weeks, does it all seem like a blur?

Make Time is here to help you make the most of your time and make everyday more meaningful, productive, and enjoyable.

Scroll down to know how.


This website, Make Time, is inspired by the book "Make Time" by authors Jake Knapp and John Zeratsky. There are 2 main things that make our lives seem so monotonous and boring:

(1) Busy Bandwagon (Endless Tasks)

The belief brought onto us by society that if we're not busy at all times, i.e. working super hard all the time, we're doing something wrong.

(2) Infinity Pools (Endless Distractions)

Applications and services with infite amount of content that refreshes every second, that keep us distracted.

We always seem to have a lot of things we could be doing. The more we get done (by riding the busy bandwagon), the more we have to do. After riding the busy bandwagons we get distracted by the infinity pool and end up wasting our days.

How can we avoid all of this? How can we make time for the stuff that actually matters, the stuff that is important to us, the stuff that makes us grow and feel good? We can't just work harder and longer.

Instead, we need to be more intentional about our time. And for that comes in the concept of Daily Highlights . We tend to remember peak moments, specially in the long run. So if each day we achieve that peak moment, a daily highlight, our days will start to feel better and we'll feel more accomplished.

A daily highlight can be something from one of these categories:

- Urgency

- Satisfaction

- Joy

So how can this site help you put all of that into action?

Daily Highlight

Write down the task you most want to get done each day in the Daily Highlight section with a planned time. During the planned time, work with laser focus to get the highlight done. Research has shown that writing down a task makes it more likely that you'll do it.

If you get the thing that means most to you everyday, in the long term your life will feel amazing.

Might-Do List

Basically a todo list but called 'might-do' to prevent the feeling of guilt if you don't get everything done. We can't be super productive everyday. If each day we get our daily some tasks from the might-do list in addition to the all important daily highlight, we'll make great progress in the long term and each day will feel great.



Activities Suggestor

Bored? Can't think of a daily highlight? Or done with the daily highlight but don't have anything else to do? Let our activity suggestor help!

Choose from the list of given topics on the activity suggestor page and we'll suggest a fun activity for you to do.
